Week 14: Finishing Touches & Presentation

Support Theory

Finishing touches turn a good build into a great one. Check for glue marks, rough edges or scratches and fix them. Add handles, rubber feet or name plates neatly. Present your carry‑all clean and dust‑free so others can see the quality of your work.

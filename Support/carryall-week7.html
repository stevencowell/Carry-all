Week 7: Hardware — Screws, Hinges & Handles

Support Theory

Hardware is the metal you add to timber. Screws, nails, hinges and handles must be chosen and fitted correctly. Screws grip better than nails and can be removed if you need to fix something later. Always drill a pilot hole slightly smaller than the screw to prevent the wood from splitting. Check screw length so it reaches into, but not through, the second piece. Good handle placement keeps the carry‑all balanced and comfortable to hold.

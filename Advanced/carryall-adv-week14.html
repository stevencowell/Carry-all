Advanced Theory: Coating Technology & Curing Systems

Industrial finishes include UV‑cured acrylates, which polymerise in <3 s under 365 nm lamps, achieving hardness >H on the pencil scale. Catalysed conversion varnish contains acid catalysts, providing high chemical resistance. Transfer efficiency of HVLP spray guns can exceed 70 %. Viscosity is measured with a Ford cup; temperature adjustment compensates for 2 % viscosity change per °C. Orange‑peel defects result from improper atomisation or over‑thick film build.
